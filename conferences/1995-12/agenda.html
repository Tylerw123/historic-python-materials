GUI SIG meeting

The GUI SIG meeting isn't a part of the Workshop proper. The goal of the meeting is to work towards a common Python interface for GUI programming. Everyone is invited to join in on the meeting, but it will probably only be of interest to the developers of GUI modules. The meeting will not be held at USGS.

Location: At CNRI, 1895 Preston White Drive, Reston, Va, in the big conference room.
